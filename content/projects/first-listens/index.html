A Field of Flowers

Since 2018*, I've recorded every album (either LP or EP) that I've listened to for the first time.

This visualisation shows the number of albums that I listened to each day by the size of the petals. Each flower is a different month.

Click on a flower to zoom in, then click on the petals to explore that day's albums. Select the "highlight favourites" option to show days with albums that I'd recommend!

*I've actually been doing this since 2017, but during that first year I didn't record the date that I listened to anything.
