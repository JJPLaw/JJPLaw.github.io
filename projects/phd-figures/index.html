Microbiological Figures

It was during my PhD in Microbiology that I found a love for designing and making data visualisations, where figures are essential for conveying the results. While many scientists are content to create visualisations that are purely functional I much preferred to put some consideration into the look and feel of a figure, as in my opinion there's really nothing worse than an ugly looking graph.

Here, I show a selection of my favourite figures, taken from:

  • My thesis, titled "The effects of subinhibitory antibiotic concentrations on Pseudomonas aeruginosa within model Cystic Fibrosis bacterial communities";
  • My subsequent published paper, titled "The Effects of Antibiotic Combination Treatments on Pseudomonas aeruginosa Tolerance Evolution and Coexistence with Stenotrophomonas maltophilia".
    • (Law, Wood & Friman. Microbiol Spectr. 2022; 10(6):e0184222)
