First Listens

Wednesday, 1st January 2025

A Field of Flowers

Since 2018*, I've recorded every album (either LP or EP) that I've listened to for the first time. Over those 6 years, that's a total of 2,257 albums (147 of which were EPs).

This visualisation shows the number of albums that I listened to each day by the size of the petals. Each flower is a different month.

Click on a flower to zoom in, then click on the petals to explore that day's albums. Select the "highlight favourites" option to show days with albums that I'd recommend!

You can read more about the development of this visualisation on my blog.

*I've actually been doing this since 2017, bringing the full tally to 2,534 albums (as of 1st Jan, 25), but during that first year I didn't record the date that I listened to anything.
